Important information: Before downloading the Biogenic Habitat Map Package please read the following information and data use agreement

The Deep Sea Coral National Geodatabase is a collection of recorded observations of deep-sea coral and sponge locations and associated information. The records are collated from multiple sources, including research by NOAA's Deep Sea Coral Research and Technology Program, NOAA trawl surveys, published scientific literature, museum collections, and other databases. It is also important to note that the database is a result of inconsistent survey effort and that coral presence is driven in part by survey effort distribution. Users should refer to the original websites or cited literature for individual data records for appropriate contextual, explanatory and interpretive information, and for relevant metadata. Questions about the Deep Sea Coral National Geodatabase itself should be directed to Dan.Dorfman@noaa.gov, NOAA Deep Sea Coral Research and Technology Program. This dataset is not to be distributed by parties outside the Deep Sea Coral Research and Technology Program. Please contact the program for the most up-to-date version.

Data Use Agreement and Recommended Citation
By using data or other information accessed through the Deep Sea Coral National Geodatabase, the user agrees that, in any publication or presentation of any sort based wholly or in part on material so accessed, they will:

1. Acknowledge the use of specific records from contributing databases in the form appearing in the 'Citation' field thereof (if any); In most cases, the reference in the database should be the citation used when the data are discussed in other publications. When referring to a derivative of this database (for example, a map showing distribution of a particular coral species), please cite this database as follows:

Deep Sea Coral National Geodatabase (NOAA Deep Sea Coral Research and Technology Program). [date accessed]
